Chicken Tortilla Soup

This is a chicken is a chicken soup with shredded chicken, black beans , and corn is quick to make, full of flavor, and very filling! it's garnished with chopped fresh avocado, Monterey Jack cheese, crushed tortilla tortilla chips, and green onion. this soup freezes well.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1(28 ounce)can crushed tomatoes
  • 1(10.5)can condensed chicken broth
  • 11/4 cups water
  • 1/2 cup crushed tortilla chips, or to taste
  • 2 medium avocadoes, sliced, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onions, or to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a stockpot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; saute until soft, about 5 minutes. Stir in crushed tomatoes, condensed broth, water, chili powder, and oregano; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 to 10 minutes.
  2. Stir black beans, cooked chicken, corn, hominy, chile peppers, and cilantro. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Ladle soup into individual serving bowls, and top with crushed tortilla ships, avocado slices, Monterey Jack cheese and green onions.
